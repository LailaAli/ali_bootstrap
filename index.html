IT'S ALWAYS SUNNY OVERVIEW




It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia is an American sitcom that began airing on FX on August 4, 2005, created by Rob McElhenney, Glenn Howerton, and Charlie Day. The first eleven seasons compose 123 episodes and three more seasons have been ordered by FXX. On the most basic level, the show is about a group of friends who run Paddy's Pub in Philadelphia. But it's much more than that - "the gang" takes on social and political issues in their own deprived way, often to awkwardly disastrous results. Their warped antics and narcissistic points of view get them in trouble with rival bars, family members, other Philadelphians, gangs, drug lords, and the authorities.

The gang is composed of the egotistical Mac (Rob McElhenney) who never misses an opportunity to stop an injustice; the illiterate Charlie (Charlie Day) whose undying obsession with a local waitress never steers him right; the eternally vain Dennis (Glenn Howerton)who never misses an opportunity to bang; his sister Dee (Kaitlin Olson) whose pursuit for fame and acceptance has yet to succeed, and their father Frank (Danny DeVito) who, after getting divorced, decides to throw caution to the wind and live without inhibitions.
It’s Always Sunny in Philadelphia was created by Rob McElhenney.


MEET THE GANG




Mac

Ronald "Mac" McDonald is a co-owner and self proclaimed "Sheriff" of Paddy's Pub and generally the pub's most active manager. He is roommates with his best friend from high school, Dennis Reynolds, and has been best friends with Charlie Kelly since childhood. Mac is a member of The Gang which also includes Dee and Frank.


Dennis

Dennis Reynolds is the co-owner of Paddy's Pub, and Dee Reynolds's fraternal twin brother. Dennis is best friends with Charlie and Mac. Dennis is extremely vain, selfish, superficial, hypersexual, and delusional about himself. Dennis also has an extreme temper. Dennis is frequently hinted to be a sociopath incapable of feelings.


Dee

Deandra "Sweet Dee" Reynolds is the bartender at Paddy's Pub. She is the twin sister of Dennis Reynolds, and the legal daughter of Frank and Barbara Reynolds. Dee considers herself a member of The Gang that runs Paddy's, although the rest of the gang often disagrees with this assertion. She rarely has success with men and she is an aspiring actress.


Charlie

Charlie Kelly is the janitor of Paddy's Pub. He is a member of the Gang that works at the bar, and is considered the "wild card" of the group. Charlie has been best friends with Mac since childhood. He shares an apartment with Frank, who is very likely his biological father. The most tedious and disgusting janitorial tasks at the bar are referred to as Charlie Work.


Frank

Franklin "Frank" Reynolds (aka; The Warthog) is the father of Dennis Reynolds and Dee Reynolds, and the ex-husband of Barbara Reynolds. Frank also lives with his apparent biological son (and ex-husband), Charlie Kelly. He used to be prim and proper, but after he divorced Barbara, he let himself lose all convictions and live with no moral compass.
